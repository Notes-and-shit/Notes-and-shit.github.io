    • For 11 days Napoleon’s section has been progressing relentlessly

    • One step at a time, despite their heavy losses the Canadians persisted against the German defenses at Passchendaele.

    • But Napoleon voted for the liberal party. He was wrong to enlist and wants to prevent other young people like him from going through hell.

    • “Frenchy help me,” Ted screams.

      • Bouvier snaps out of it
