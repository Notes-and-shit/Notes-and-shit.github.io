    L5 - Arithmetic and Geometric Series

    Jun 11, 20244 min read

    What are series?

    A series is the sum of terms of a sequence of numbers

    Arithmetic sequence: Sum of terms in a arithmetic sequence

    Geometric sequence: Sum of terms in a geometric sequence

    Finding series

    To find an arithmetic series:

    Sn​=2n(a+tn​)​
    (if the value of tn​ is known)

    Sn​=2n(2a+(n−1)d)​
    (if the value of tn​ is unknown)

    To find a geometric series:

    Sn​=r−1a(rn−1)​,r=1
