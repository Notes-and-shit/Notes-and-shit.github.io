    Notes

    What is a geometric sequence?

    A geometric sequence is a sequence in which the ratio between two consecutive terms is constant.

    We can always multiply/divide the same value to find the next term.

    Key Formula

    For a geometric sequence the formula for a general term is:

    tn​=a∗rn−1

    a=first term t1​
    r=the common ratio
    n=term number
