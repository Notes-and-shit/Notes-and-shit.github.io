    What is a rational exponent?

    A rational exponent is an exponent that is a rational number

    Ex: x21​

    Rules for rational exponents

    xnm​ = nxm​ or (n(​x))m

    If n is even, negative radicands are impossible to solve

    4−16​=impossible

    If n is odd, negative radicands are possible to solve

    3−27​=−3
