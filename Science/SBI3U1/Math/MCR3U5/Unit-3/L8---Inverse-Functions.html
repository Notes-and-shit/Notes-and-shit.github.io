    L8 - Inverse Functions

    Nov 23, 20242 min read

    What is an inverse of a relation?

    An inverse relation is a reflection of a relation relative to y=x.

    In an inverse relation:

    • The domain becomes the image, and the image becomes the domain

    3 methods of identifying an inverse

    Method 1: Table of values

    1. Take the table of values of and flip every value of x to y and every value of y to x
    2. Turn the domain into the image and the image into the domain

    Method 2: Graph

    1. Trace the inverse of the relation by switching the x and y values

    Method 3: Equation

    1. Switch the values of x and y in the equation
    2. Isolate y
